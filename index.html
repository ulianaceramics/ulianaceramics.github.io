Hi, my name is Uliana!

I was born and raised in Kyiv which I love endlessly. For now, my field of interest has no limits and ceramics represents this wildly insane research. Ceramics and me are together since 2014. Graduated from the National Academy of Fine Arts and Architecture with a specialty in graphics, workshop M. Kompanets, I achieved a Specialist degree. I studied at the Summer Academy of Pottery in the National Museum of Ukrainian Pottery in the village of Opishnaya 2019.
